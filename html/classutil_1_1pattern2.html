Paint the passed pattern on top of this pattern at the given location and with the given opacity.

The passed x, y coordinates specify the location on this pattern where the other pattern's upper-left corner will appear. The passed pattern will be cropped as needed to ensure that it is not painted outside the bounds of this pattern. E.g., you can paint a 100x100 pattern over a 10x10 pattern without issue. The opacity is in the range 0 to 1. An opacity of 1 overwrites this pattern entirely with the new one. Opacity values less than 1 use a weighted average of the two patterns to perform blending. The opacity is not clamped.
