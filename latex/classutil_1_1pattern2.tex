{\footnotesize\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{classutil_1_1vec3}{void}} util\+::pattern2\+::paint (\begin{DoxyParamCaption}\item[{\mbox{\hyperlink{classutil_1_1pattern2}{pattern2}}}]{p,  }\item[{\mbox{\hyperlink{classutil_1_1vec3}{float}}}]{opacity = {\ttfamily 1},  }\item[{\mbox{\hyperlink{classutil_1_1vec3}{int}}}]{x = {\ttfamily 0},  }\item[{\mbox{\hyperlink{classutil_1_1vec3}{int}}}]{y = {\ttfamily 0} }\end{DoxyParamCaption})}



Paint the passed pattern on top of this pattern at the given location and with the given opacity. 

The passed x, y coordinates specify the location on this pattern where the other pattern\textquotesingle{}s upper-\/left corner will appear. The passed pattern will be cropped as needed to ensure that it is not painted outside the bounds of this pattern. E.\+g., you can paint a 100x100 pattern over a 10x10 pattern without issue. The opacity is in the range 0 to 1. An opacity of 1 overwrites this pattern entirely with the new one. Opacity values less than 1 use a weighted average of the two patterns to perform blending.
